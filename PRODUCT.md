# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Primary visitors are shoe factory owners, the business owner, and footwear brand clients who need a credible partner for manufacturing management improvement, delivery control, quality stability, cost reduction, and factory upgrade decisions.

## Product Purpose

The website presents Putian Zhengqi as a professional shoe-industry consulting company. It should help visitors understand the company's practical consulting approach, review service directions and cases, and start a low-friction consultation with confidence.

## Brand Personality

Professional, minimalist, trustworthy. The tone should feel calm, capable, practical, and grounded in real factory operations.

## Anti-references

Avoid exaggerated, fancy, cluttered, overly decorative, or template-like visual styles. Avoid noisy gradients, excessive icons, inflated claims, and visuals that feel disconnected from manufacturing work.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with operational credibility: connect every visual and message to real factory management outcomes.
2. Keep decisions easy: make services, cases, and consultation paths clear without overwhelming visitors.
3. Use restraint with confidence: polished layouts, balanced spacing, and deliberate color should create trust.
4. Show improvement as a process: diagnostics, roadmap, implementation, and review should feel connected.
5. Make contact feel low-risk: the bottom of every page should invite a practical first conversation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for body text and key controls. Maintain readable Chinese typography, visible focus states, reduced-motion fallbacks, and clear labels for interactive elements.
